Welcome to HackShop

Hack Shop is an organization where students' interest drive their progress in a self-paced environment that focuses on the many forms that technology can take in our young people’s lives and empowers them to “hack” it to their liking. In our learning environments, hacking isn’t a bad word or a criminal activity, it is the process of taking disparate pieces of information or a diverse set of tools and combining them in a new way to create new knowledge and solutions.

Our Programs

A demonstration sequence of activities for the Big Shoulders STEM Scholars Program 8th-Grade Class. Groups of 20 students meet every other week for 2 hours of instruction on Saturday mornings.
